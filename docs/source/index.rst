=================
Django pony forms
=================

*Django pony forms* is an extension to the Django form. It helps you to produce better html for your forms,
and to configure the html output.

The source is on github: https://github.com/mbraak/django_pony_forms

What can *Django pony forms* do for you?
========================================

**Better form html by default**
  Just add *PonyFormMixin* to your form class.

**Form templates**
  *Django pony forms* allows you to write form templates. You can write a template for all your forms, or a specific template for one particular form.
